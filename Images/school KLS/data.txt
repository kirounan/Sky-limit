King Integrated American School, located in 10 km Borg Al Arab Road, was established in 2015 to provide King Mariout Residents with an excellent quality Education. 
KIAS was one of the most well-known Schools in King Mariout area, 
and in 2018 it became one of Kaumeya Language School Branches.
